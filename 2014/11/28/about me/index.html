about me

xr h
2014-11-28

Welcome to Rui’s homepage. I am a 22-year-old Chinese, with all curiosity to this fantastic world.

Days before, I set up this blog by myself. I’d like to share my story and thoughts here. I’ve written blogs on blog.sina.com.cn, which is a Chinese blog platform. But I don’t really like it because of the annoying ads. So I set up one of my own, with my own domain and hosting, and it’s proudly powered by Wordpress.

I like reading, playing soccer, listening to music, swimming, learning to code, learning English and so on.

I’ve read some very good books recently, some of them are useful, some of them are inspiring and thought – provoking. I’d like to recommend The Fountainhead authored by Ayn Rand. It makes me seriously think about what should I pursuit in my life.

My favorite soccer player is Messi. As you might know, he is the most excellent player in the world. His passion for soccer and life inspires me a lot. As for clubs, I like the 2 FCBs, MU, MC, Arsenal .etc. Actually, I like most of the players and teams who play good soccer. I think that’s because I essentially love soccer very much.

I began to listen to English songs just in March this year, I really should have done this long before. I‘ve found there are so many good songs. My favorite artists are: Coldplay, P!nk, Linkin Park, Katy Perry, Carrie Underwood, Lana del Rey, Sam Tsui and whoever produces amazing songs!

I like to learn English cuz I found it really useful. I have seen a much vaster world through this language. But as you can see, my English is still mediocre. I am trying to improve my English.

Btw, Rui is a Chinese pinyin which has the same pronunciation as “Ray”.
